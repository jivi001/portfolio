The Challenge

Manual internship allocation for 500+ candidates across 50+ positions was taking 2 weeks, resulting in suboptimal matches and low satisfaction rates. The process involved multiple stakeholders, conflicting preferences, and complex criteria that were difficult to balance manually.

The Solution

Developed an intelligent ML-based allocation engine that considers multiple factors:

  • Skill Matching: Cosine similarity between candidate skills and role requirements
  • Preference Optimization: Multi-objective optimization balancing candidate and organizational preferences
  • Fair Distribution: Ensures diverse team compositions and equitable allocation

Technical Implementation

Next.js Python Scikit-learn Flask MongoDB

Business Impact

Recognized at Smart India Hackathon 2025. Reduced allocation time by 95% while improving match quality. Participants reported significantly better role-skill alignment and higher satisfaction with their assignments.
